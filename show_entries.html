How to complete

  • Press the generate random sentences button
  • For each sentence, check the appropriate sentiment boxes. (A sentence CANNOT be both Positive & Negative or both Strong & Weak).
    We would like for you to grade the sentences based on how they are written, rather than what they say.
    * If you don't think the sentence has any sentiment, leave all checkboxes BLANK
  • After you have classified each sentence, press the submit button.
  • If you would like to keep classifying, press the generate random sentences button and repeat.
